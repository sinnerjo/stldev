About STL Soccer Dev Lab

A personal project focused on helping St Louis players and coaches grow through enjoyment, creativity, and intelligent football.

Purpose

STL Soccer Dev Lab exists to give players, coaches, and families in St Louis a clear framework for development. It is not a club or academy. It is a learning space built around a consistent philosophy and game model.

What makes this space different

Fun and engaging
Training and match ideas are designed to be enjoyable and energetic. Players should look forward to being on the ball and being with their team.
Long term focus
The project looks beyond this weekend to where a player can be in several years. Growth is measured by decisions, habits, and love for the game.
Individual skills first
While team play matters, individual skill and understanding are the foundation. The aim is to build players who can fit into many systems and styles.

Coaching identity behind the lab

The project reflects a coaching personality that is positive, engaging, and attacking.

Training favors the ball, attacking intent, and moments that require players to make choices. The coach role is to guide, challenge, and support rather than control every action on the field.
