Help, how to use MatchTap

Modes

Player tracks one kid, no player selection needed, just tap events. Team whole tracks the team as a group with one tap per event, no roster required. Team individual has you tap a player chip first, then the event, giving per player detail at the cost of an extra tap.

Opponent

Type the other team's name in Setup. It shows in the header while tracking and labels the game once it's saved to Past games.

Events to track

Check the boxes for whatever you want live on the Track screen. Everything unchecked stays out of the way, you can turn events on or off any time from Setup.

Score

A scoreboard appears automatically at the top of Track, but only once both Goal and Goal conceded are checked. It counts those two buttons directly, nothing else.

Game clock

The clock bar on Track is also the start button. Tap it once to begin the game. Tap it again any time to pause, tap again to resume, it remembers exactly where it left off. The Show game clock setting only controls whether the running time is visible, the bar still starts and pauses the game either way.

Tracking events

In Team individual mode, tap a player chip to select who you're tracking, then tap an event button. Undo last removes the most recent tap if you hit the wrong one. The 1st half and 2nd half buttons just tag new events, they don't affect the clock.

Log tab

Shows totals and the full event history for the current game, with a CSV export. Save game to history archives everything here into Past games and clears the board so you're ready for the next match.

Past games tab

Every saved game lives here. Tap a card to see its full event list, export that single game to CSV, or delete it.

Reset current game

Wipes the game in progress, events, score, and clock, without saving anything. Use this for a mis-start, not for ending a real game, that's what Save game to history is for.
