Resources

Printable tools and templates that support the philosophy and game model. You can adapt these for your team or club in the St Louis area.

For coaches

Session planning template
A simple layout for goals, key coaching points, activities, and reflection. Keeps the session connected to the player pathway and game model.
Principles of play one page
A single sheet view of the main ideas with and without the ball, plus key transition cues.

For players

Player self check
A short self reflection sheet that asks about effort, decision making, creativity, and enjoyment each week.
Personal goals sheet
A simple tool for players to set one or two goals for the next few weeks and track progress.

For families

What to watch for on game day
A guide for parents on how to notice development behaviors such as decisions, effort, and attitude, not just the final score.
Questions for the car ride home
A list of gentle, player centered questions that help kids reflect without pressure after games.

As the project grows, this page can host downloadable PDFs, diagrams, and links to videos or presentations.
