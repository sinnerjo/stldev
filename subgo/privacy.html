Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 10, 2026

SubGo does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any server. All data you enter stays on your device.

Overview

SubGo ("the app") is a sideline substitution and game tracker for soccer coaches, developed by Joseph Sinner and available at stldev.com/subgo and on the Google Play Store. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data We Collect

SubGo does not collect any personal information. The app does not require an account, email address, or any identifying information to use.

Data Stored on Your Device

SubGo stores the following information locally on your device using your browser's local storage:

  • Team names you create
  • Player names and jersey numbers you enter
  • Game history including scores, substitutions, and minutes played
  • App settings and preferences

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party. It is not accessible to the developer or anyone else.

Data Sharing

SubGo does not share any data with third parties. There are no analytics services, advertising networks, or data brokers involved in the operation of this app.

Analytics

The SubGo website at stldev.com uses Google Analytics to measure general page traffic (number of visitors, general geographic region, device type). This is standard website analytics and does not include any data you enter into the app itself. No player names, team names, or game data are ever sent to Google Analytics.

Advertising

SubGo contains no advertisements.

Children's Privacy

SubGo is intended for use by coaches and adults. The app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Player names entered into the app are stored locally on the device only and are never transmitted anywhere.

Data Deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by:

  • Using the Clear All and Clear History options within the app
  • Clearing your browser's site data for stldev.com in your device settings
  • Uninstalling the app

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date. Significant changes will be noted in the app's release notes.
